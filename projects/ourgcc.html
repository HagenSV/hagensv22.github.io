OurGCC (2024)

Languages/Tools: Swift

Purpose: The goal of the project was to work with a partner to develop a helpful iOS app to demonstrate the skills that you learned in the class. My partner and I chose to develop and app to help GCC students get information about the college campus. The app displayed information about campus groups, campus events, building hours, and meals. It solved the problem of this information previously being difficult to come by, with group information only being available by word of mouth or annual Organization Fair. The app also allowed students to review their meals at the dining halls allowing other students to make more informed decisions about where to eat on campus.
